Features:
1) CRUD
2) Auto create Tag/Category while creating article if tag is not available and assign foreign key automatically
3) Update and Delete in Tag table will be effect in Article table foreign key and tag column
4) Article search by author, date, tag, title, id. 
5) Bookmark article for later reading, remove, show bookmarks by user
6) Trending news Section / Last N trending news / Trending news by date
7) Share on social media / get shares by user/ get shares by platform / delete share
8) Exported CSV of article
